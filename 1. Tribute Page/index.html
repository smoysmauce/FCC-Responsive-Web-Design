End-to-End Product Cycle

A short guide

RebelDot's end-to-end software development process

RebelDot's software development process is quite good. From the initial analysis and discovery stage, the team moves onto the User Experience design stage, in which they create UX artifacts to understand and grasp the scope of their work. They then move onto the development of the software itself, first creating an MVP (Minimal Viable Product), then developing from there.
